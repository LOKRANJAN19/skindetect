Understanding Skin Cancer

Skin cancer is one of the most common types of cancer and occurs when abnormal cells in the skin grow uncontrollably. It is primarily classified into three main types: basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and melanoma.

Skin cancer can have significant impacts on health and quality of life. Early detection and treatment are crucial for effective management and improving outcomes. Understanding the risk factors, symptoms, and preventive measures can help reduce the incidence of skin cancer and promote overall skin health.
